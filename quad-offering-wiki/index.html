Part D

What the delta tells us

Synthesis

The strategic read on the coverage map

Coverage is strong where data is public (fees, curriculum, admission process, scholarships, loans) and weak where it needs Quad-team facts (placement data, named faculty, degree-grantor per program, seats, FAQ answers, hostel, refund, dates). The offering is input-bound, not research-bound.

Highest-value Tier-1 gaps — all Quad-owned

  • Degree-granting partner + its accreditation, per program
  • Real placement statistics (institution + program level)
  • FAQ answers — especially the Degree-Recognition-&-Validity bucket
  • Selection / QAT mechanics (syllabus, dates, cutoffs)

What Recurr can close without the client (🟡)

  • FAQ bucket scaffolding (the 13-bucket structure)
  • Student life (from /life-at-quad) · campus pages (from /gallery)
