§1

The Idea

On the record

The world's most charming bureaucracy. We write and look like a real government ministry — Cabinet, circulars, policy, citizens, decrees — applied to oats and mornings, with a completely straight face.

The most nonchalant, unserious ministry that ever existed: heavy dry sarcasm, but the design stays calm, cool, and toned down. The humour comes from gravity and deadpan — never from being loud. We do not announce that we are fun. The straight face does that for us.

The Voice

Two engines

Article 1 — Principles of address

  1. iStraight face, always. No exclamation marks were harmed.
  2. iiWe never say we're fun — the deadpan does it.
  3. iiiMaximum gravity, minimum stakes.
  4. ivThe wordplay is the wink, never the whole joke.
  5. vReal officialese over jokey filler.

Two engines of language

Officialese builds the world — citizens, registrations, constituencies, circulars headed Subject: / Ref:, all closing “By order of the Cabinet.”

The oat wordplay supplies the wit, deadpan and sparing: It's oat-ficial, the Oath of Oats, oat-ority, devoated citizens, the Senoate.
