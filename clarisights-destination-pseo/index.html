What it is

What Looker Studio is

Looker Studio is Google's free, browser-based dashboard tool. Marketing teams use it to build shared reports that pull data from across the stack — Google Ads, GA4, Sheets, BigQuery — and embed them in everything from CMO updates to client deliverables.

The catch: Looker Studio is only as good as the data flowing in. Most teams end up wiring 10-15 individual connectors, each with its own schema, its own bugs, and its own fees. Clarisights replaces that mess with one normalized, monitored feed — so every chart in Looker Studio reads from the same source of truth.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I still need individual Looker Studio connectors after I plug in Clarisights?
No. The Clarisights connector replaces every per-platform connector you'd otherwise wire up — one feed instead of 15 to manage, debug, and pay for.
How does my data actually land in Looker Studio?
Clarisights provides a native Looker Studio connector. Add it as a data source, choose the metrics and dimensions you want, and your unified marketing data is available in any chart, table, or scorecard.
Is the data always fresh, or do I have to refresh manually?
Clarisights syncs from every source on an hourly schedule. Your Looker Studio dashboards pull the latest data on every load — no manual refresh, no scheduled extracts.
Why not just use Looker Studio's free native connectors?
They cover one platform each, with raw fields and zero monitoring. The moment you need cross-channel ROAS or a metric Looker Studio doesn't ship out of the box, you're back to manual data prep. Clarisights handles all of that upstream so the dashboard layer stays clean.
What's the lift to migrate from a tool we're already paying for?
For most teams, under a week. We help map your existing dashboards to Clarisights fields, run the two systems in parallel for a sprint, then cut over once parity is confirmed.
