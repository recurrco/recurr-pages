Always

Pair the Latin and Devanagari. Vayam is bilingual; the wordmark behaves that way. The Devanagari sits as a partner, never a decoration.

When inverted

The disc reverses (paper on ink). The wordmark goes paper. The Devanagari subtitle goes 60% paper. Nothing else changes.

Never

Outline the disc. Apply gradients. Place over busy photography without a backplate. Rotate the wordmark. Substitute the Devanagari for English in the lockup.

04 · Images & Watermark

Three kinds of image. Nothing else. Every one carries the mark.

A Vayam article is mostly type and image. Stock photography, decorative graphics, generic stock illustrations — none of these appear. We use three kinds of image and we treat all three the same way.

व vayam

Hyperreal Render A rendering of India's first sovereign GPU cluster. Vayam, 2026.
व vayam
Chart · Data Viz India's installed renewable capacity, FY20–FY26.

Watermark rule. The disc-mark + wordmark sits 14px from the bottom-right of every published image, rendered in white at full opacity over photography (mix-blend-mode: difference) and ink over light surfaces. It is the size of the caption beneath it. It is never optional. On chart and illustration tiles, the watermark gets a tinted backplate to stay legible.

06 · The Publishing Cycle

One essay is the source code. Four channels are the compile targets.

Every Vayam story begins as a long, deeply-reported essay on vayam.in. That essay is the master document. From it, four channel teams adapt their own native cuts for Instagram, YouTube, X, and LinkedIn. The essay is never a derivative of social. Social is always a derivative of the essay.
