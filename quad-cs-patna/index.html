FAQ

Questions students & parents ask

Who awards the degree? Is it recognised?
Quad delivers the industry-led programme and skilling; your degree is awarded by our partner university. Quad AI (Onenorth Learning Pvt. Ltd.) is a training institute, not a UGC-regulated HEI — so you get a recognised degree and an industry-ready skillset.
Why choose Quad Patna over leaving Bihar for a metro college?
You get the same MAANG-mentor, build-real-products education — without relocating an 18-year-old, and at a fraction of the cost of a metro (₹8L all-in for four years vs. lakhs a year on hostel + fees elsewhere). The talent is already here; now the classroom is too.
What placement support do I actually get?
Dedicated 1:1 career coaching, resume reviews and mock interviews with MAANG engineers, a mandatory 6-month paid internship, and access to a 75+ company hiring network. We’re early — so we’re transparent: we sell support and access, not a placement statistic.
What does the ₹8L cover, and is EMI available?
It’s all-in for the full four years — tuition, software, development and experiential learning. Scholarships up to 50% are available, and EMI / education loans through our finance partners.
Is there accommodation for outstation students?
Yes — your counsellor will walk you and your family through campus, accommodation and commute options for Patna on the call, so you know exactly what day-to-day looks like before you decide.
What is the QAT, and how hard is it?
A 60-minute online aptitude test covering logical reasoning, data interpretation, quantitative ability and communication. It’s built to find potential and fit — not to trip you up.
