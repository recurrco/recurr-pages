§ 01 — The Idea

We write and look like a real government ministry — and we apply every bit of it to a bowl of oats.

Cabinet meetings about granola. Circulars about joy. Citizens who have taken an oath. The humour is the contrast between the weight of the institution and the smallness of what it governs — never volume, never a wink that lasts too long.

This direction takes that solemn machinery and sets it inside the warmest, calmest, most grown-up-playful surface we could build. A government department, reimagined by a lovely modern food brand.
