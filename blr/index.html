02 — Voice of the Internet

voice.blr.store

The research platform. A subdomain hosting the product intelligence engine. Go category by category, find all products, create profiles, aggregate sentiment from across the internet. Programmatically built pages for search volume capture.

What It Does

  • Product profiles with metadata tags
  • Brand profiles with full portfolio
  • Aggregate sentiment: Amazon, Flipkart, Nykaa, Reddit, YouTube
  • Open-ended comparison: pull any 2 products, compare side-by-side
  • SEO + LLM optimized programmatic pages
  • Reddit community owned by BLR for crowdsourced intelligence

Ranking Methodology

  • Star ratings (aggregated cross-platform)
  • Review count (volume signal)
  • Best seller tags (marketplace signal)
  • UGC sentiment (Reddit, YouTube, social)

Price and platform availability are separate filter vectors, not ranking inputs. Refresh cycle: every 3–4 months per category.

Primary channel: Reddit

06 — Principles

What we care about when we curate

Longevity

Products that last. We don't feature disposable hype. If it breaks in a month, it doesn't make the shelf.

Design Premium

Someone thought about this. Intentional choices in form, function, packaging, and experience.

Ingredient Premium

What's in it matters. Better ingredients, cleaner formulations, transparent sourcing.

Quality Premium

Durability, build, longevity. The kind of product you recommend to a friend without hesitation.

The Test

If we wouldn't buy it ourselves, it doesn't go on the shelf. Simple.

Price Is Not Quality

A ₹200 product that's better built beats a ₹2,000 product that isn't. Premium is about the product, not the price tag.
