The thesis

MOO can be a ₹100 Cr+ brand. Not a breakfast brand. Not a health brand. A food brand with a fundamentally different take on F&B — one where tech, design, and a low-cost scaling strategy are the moat, not the marketing.

The last QSR to go global while staying absolutely lean — and while making its operating design the brand — was Subway. We are building in that tradition. Standardised kitchens, modular menus, a software spine, and a consumer experience that feels designed rather than assembled.

The business design is the edge. It is also what produces the extraordinary margins that make the numbers work.

Three objectives

Objective 01 · Expand

Grow the store footprint.

We cannot be a national brand from three cloud kitchens. The only path to expansion is capital — which means this quarter is a fundraise quarter, end to end.

KR 1
Close a seed round by June 15.In market May 15. Target investors briefed and meeting by May 20. LoI by end-May. Close by June 15.
KR 2
Three existing stores show WoW growth for 6 consecutive weeks.No story works if the base isn't growing. HSR, Domlur, Whitefield on a WoW-positive trajectory before the deck goes out.
KR 3
Signed LoI from Urban Vault (or equivalent anchor) on the retail kiosk plan.The expansion story needs a believable next-store. Kiosk model, anchor partner, ready to execute post-close.
Objective 02 · Yield

Cross ₹10 Cr revenue in FY27.

The fundraise buys new stores. The ₹10 Cr number has to come from the stores we already have — which means maximising throughput and yield inside each kitchen before we add any new ones.

KR 1
Per-store exit run-rate of ₹10 L / month by end of Q1.Across aggregators + direct + events. Three stores × ₹10 L = ₹30 L/mo exit. Puts FY27 on a ₹3.6 Cr base before new-store additions.
KR 2
Direct margin stays at 31% or better, aggregator blended at 25%.No yield-at-any-cost. Hold unit economics as volume grows.
KR 3
Ship Platform v1 (store module) + central kitchen module.The Outpost platform is the yield lever. If managers can't see what's missing, they miss it. Ship before May 15 so investors see software shipping, not software planned.
Objective 03 · Brand

Scale the brand to the point investors recognise it.

Before May 15 an investor in Bangalore should either already know MOO, or know someone who does. Brand is a fundraise input, not a fundraise output.

KR 1
5,000 Instagram followers by June 30.From the current base. Earned through content, not boosts.
KR 2
3,000 direct orders / month run-rate by end of Q1.The direct channel is where brand becomes revenue. MOO at X partners, ministryofoats.com, canteen bookings — all feeding one number.
KR 3
Three earned-media mentions in BLR press or creator circles.One food creator, one business publication, one neighbourhood publication. Recognition is a compounding asset.

Gates before we go to market

None of the three objectives hold if these aren't true on May 15. They are the pre-flight checks for the fundraise.

  1. Three stores growing WoW, consistently. Six consecutive weeks of WoW-positive topline at HSR, Domlur, Whitefield. Abhi owns the number.
  2. Brand presence online and in BLR. Instagram on trajectory to 5K, ministryofoats.com shipped with real content, at least one earned mention in the bag. Rushil owns.
  3. Investors have heard of us. Warm intros lined up. Deck tested on three friendly investors before it goes out cold. Pratik owns.
  4. Team is structured. JDs, OKRs and reporting lines locked for Abhi, Rushil and Manju. An investor should see an org, not a founder with helpers.
  5. Software is shipping. Outpost v1 store module live in all three kitchens. Platform is the thing that makes the ops story credible.

What we are not doing this quarter

A quarter this tight is defined as much by what we cut as by what we commit to.

Not expanding stores ourselves. Store #4 is funded by the round, not by cashflow. No new kitchens before June 15.

Not launching new brands. TWF and Protein Police stay as menu projects inside MOO. No separate brand builds.

Not chasing paid growth. The 5K IG and 3K direct-orders numbers are earned targets. Paid is a post-raise lever.

Not rebuilding software we already have. Outpost v1 ships as designed. New modules wait for Q2.
