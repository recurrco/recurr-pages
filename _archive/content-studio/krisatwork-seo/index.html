The Opportunity

Near-zero organic presence = massive greenfield opportunity. Only 2 keywords ranking (both page 3-4). The category has winnable keywords with $30-$94 CPCs. With 26 pages and 90 days of execution, we project 239–702 organic visits/month — a 478x–1,404x increase from current state.

What We Found

  • 9 keyword categories mapped, 65+ unique keywords identified
  • 26 pages planned (6 landing, 7 comparison, 11 blog, 2 tools)
  • Comparison pages are the highest-ROI play — competitor fatigue is real (Gong, Clari, 6sense, ZoomInfo all have "alternative" search demand)
  • "SaaS fatigue" is an unowned narrative — KD 3-5, aligns perfectly with Kris thesis
  • Two domains diluting authority — consolidate krisatwork.com + krisatwork.ai immediately

Critical First Actions

  1. Consolidate domains — pick .com or .ai, 301 the other
  2. Publish 7 comparison pages in first 30 days (fastest ROI)
  3. Write the SaaS Fatigue manifesto — category-creation content
  4. Build "revenue intelligence platform" pillar page — KD 2-11, $35 CPC
  5. Get 1-2 case studies published — even anonymized enterprise stories
