The Approach

From signal to conversation.
Recurr runs it end to end.

Three steps. One objective: start qualified, contextual conversations with Krisatwork's ICP on LinkedIn — using signals they've already made public.

01

Scrape

Monitor LinkedIn for ICP posts, comments, and threads at all three pain levels — activity, use case, and strategic. We identify enterprise sales leaders who are publicly expressing the problems Krisatwork solves. Weekly signal harvest. Classified and logged.

02

Craft

Use Krisatwork's existing messaging doc to build context-matched 1:1 content. Not templates. Each response maps the specific pain signal to Krisatwork's companion positioning — anchored in Ananta's "assistant vs companion" framework and Krisatwork's two core use cases.

03

Engage

Recurr initiates and manages the 1:1 conversations on LinkedIn. We track responses, qualify intent, and surface warm conversations for the Krisatwork team. You don't touch it until it's worth your time.
