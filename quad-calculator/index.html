Assumptions & flags

  1. Revenue base for commission = Total fee − MSU share − ₹1L Macbook − Scholarship. Commission % applied to this base (2.5% B.Tech & Design, 3% BBA/MBA).
  2. Patna B.Tech uses MLR pricing (Mohit: "Patna and MLR have the exact same pricing"). Patna BBA uses BLR BBA pricing as a placeholder — flag for confirmation.
  3. Baselines are Quad's "we can deliver this without you" numbers, excluding B2B + YT-influencer admissions (those are UTM-attributed to Quad and outside the commission count).
  4. Scholarship is modelled as a uniform % reduction to the revenue base across the cohort. Real scholarship distribution is 5–50% — actual average will sit somewhere in between.
  5. 2028+ baselines not yet defined by Mohit. This calculator covers 2026 + 2027 cohorts only.
  6. Cash flow assumes equal annual spread across course years (4 for B.Tech, 3 for BBA, 2 for MBA). Real Quad collections are semester-tagged but the annual approximation is close enough for planning.
  7. Retainer billed monthly, modelled annually here. Extension to Y2+ is pure upside — left as a knob.
  8. New campuses (Hyd/Mum/Indore) + Design courses not included — baselines defined (100/50) but fees TBD.
