FAQ

Common questions

What does the first month look like?
The first 15–30 days are dedicated to building the context engine — deep research into your product, market, buyer, and competitive landscape. You'll also get the strategy layer: positioning, messaging matrix, competitive framing, and a content roadmap. After that, we start shipping artifacts weekly.
How is this different from hiring a content agency?
Content agencies produce content. We build the intelligence layer that makes content work. Every blog post, landing page, and sales deck we create is derived from a positioning system — not written from a brief. The difference shows up in conversion rates, not word counts.
What does "AI-powered" actually mean?
We maintain a living context engine — your positioning, competitive intelligence, buyer research, messaging frameworks — powered by AI systems that keep it current. When we write a battle card or a landing page, it's informed by this engine, not started from scratch. This is how one expert can deliver what typically requires a team of five.
Do you do customer interviews?
Yes. Jobs-to-be-done interviews with your customers and prospects are a core part of the context-building phase. We don't build personas from industry reports — we talk to real buyers and extract what actually drives their decisions.
What if I don't want to continue the engagement?
Everything we build is yours. Depending on the scope, it takes us 15–45 days to do a clean handoff — documenting the context engine, training your team on the systems, and if needed, helping you hire or set up automation to maintain things going forward. We recommend planning the transition so nothing falls through the cracks.
Can you work with companies outside India?
Yes. We work with B2B SaaS companies regardless of geography. Many of our engagements involve Indian companies selling to US or global buyers — a context we understand particularly well.
