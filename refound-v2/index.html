Questions

The things you're probably wondering.

Isn't this just asking ChatGPT or Perplexity what to do?+
A one-shot answer is exactly what we're not — you can already get that, and it's worth what it costs. The list is alive in four ways an essay can't be: it's grounded in your truth (your analytics, funnel and product, not the generic web), it's ranked to a specific move with a brief — not advice you still have to interpret, it refreshes as your market moves, and it learns from what actually converted. A document is stale the moment it's written.
How is this different from a content or SEO agency?+
An agency hands you a deck you wait on and bills you for their hours — the plan and the pace are theirs. ReFound is a product: the ranked plan is yours, in front of you, today, and you run the games at your pace. You pay for the intelligence and only the muscle you choose to add. Want us to play the games for you? That's a service behind the product — not the product itself.
What exactly is a "game"?+
One specific place on the internet where your buyers are deciding and you can win — a comparison query, a live thread, a listicle, a page you should own. Each game tells you why it matters and what winning looks like, ships with a brief, and has an owner who marks it done. You see them all, ranked.
Do you do the work, or just tell me what to do?+
Today, we hand you the ranked games and the briefs — you do the work, with full control. Soon you'll be able to have AI do the knowledge work for a game (you do the last mile), or pass it to a vetted expert who picks it up for you. The product is knowing exactly what to do; the doing is yours, with help available when you want it.
What do you need from us to start?+
Read access to your truth — analytics (GA4 & Search Console), your product, pricing, support docs, case studies and customers — plus one short call. The more first-party data you share, the sharper and more yours the list becomes. It's the heaviest-weighted evidence we have.
We don't really have a website yet. Can you still help?+
Yes. The engine maps your whole opportunity regardless — and plenty of games (Reddit threads, listicles, getting cited in AI answers) need no website at all. If you're starting from scratch, your first game is often standing up the right surface, and we tell you exactly what to put on it.
How long does onboarding take?+
About a week or two. We learn your market cold before handing over a single game — because a list built on a shallow read of your buyer isn't worth running.
Who is it for?+
B2B software that's found product-market fit, roughly $100K–$10M in revenue, founder-led or a small team. If you have a real product and you're not yet the answer where your buyers look, it's for you. Pre-product or enterprise — not yet.
