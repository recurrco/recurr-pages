3. Programs

Every growth initiative is designed as a program — a defined workstream with a clear rationale, expected output, and target outcome. Programs can be inbound (content, SEO), outbound (sales enablement, outreach), or strategic (positioning, partnerships). Each program has its own page with the full plan.

Program A · Inbound
The Open Standard for Paid Marketing Data

Why: Nobody owns the standard for marketing data nomenclature. Clarisights sees more cross-platform data than anyone. This is the authority play — glossary, metrics, platform guides, naming conventions, industry playbooks, and training.

Output: 260-365 pSEO reference pages (Layer 1), industry playbooks for eCommerce, Gaming, Fintech, Travel, Retail (Layer 2), training webinars and editorial series (Layer 3).

Outcome: Organic traffic foundation. Data literacy authority. Pipeline from engaged, educated audience. "We follow the Clarisights standard."

L1 April · L2 May · L3 June    Research complete — build next
 Program B · Inbound
Data & Integrations

Why: Every buyer searches for their specific platforms. "Clarisights + Google Ads" or "marketing analytics for TikTok." These are high-intent commercial queries that signal active evaluation.

Output: 370+ integration pages — data sources, destinations, connectors. Each page is report-first (what you can build with this data) rather than feature-first.

Outcome: Commercial traffic capture. Integration-aware leads. Competitive displacement (especially Funnel.io during their pricing crisis).

 Program C · Sales Enablement
Competitive Displacement

Why: Funnel.io is in active crisis (reliability, pricing). Supermetrics "alternative" is the highest-value BOFU keyword in the category ($88 CPC, KD 1). Datorama users are locked in by Salesforce contracts. Every prospect evaluates 2-3 competitors before choosing. These pages do the selling when our reps aren't in the room.

Output: 4 bespoke displacement pages (Funnel.io, Supermetrics, Datorama, Improvado) — each with a unique structure, intelligence-powered sections from real customer signals, and distribution via sales team + Reddit + SEO.

Outcome: Re-engage closed-loss Funnel deals. Intercept Supermetrics refugees. Push Datorama alternative ranking from #8 to #1. Arm every AE with competitor-specific content.
