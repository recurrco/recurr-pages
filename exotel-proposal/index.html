The CPaaS market is shifting fast.

Five signals that make the next twelve months decisive for Exotel's positioning.

01

AI is collapsing the buying journey

Buyers increasingly shortlist vendors through ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini. Companies without structured, AI-readable content are simply not in the conversation.

02

CPaaS is commoditising at speed

IVR, click-to-call, basic telephony are table stakes. Differentiation battle is now on AI-native CX, vertical depth, integrations. Messaging has to move there first.

03

Win rate is a positioning problem, not a sales problem

When deals are lost it's rarely on price. It's on inability to articulate differentiated value fast enough — a PMM gap, not a sales gap.

04

Mid-market is under-served and high-intent

India's 200–2000-seat businesses are steadily adopting cloud telephony. Most vendor content is still geared toward enterprise or SMB. The middle is exposed.

05

Analyst credibility is a GTM multiplier

Gartner, Forrester, IDC, Frost, Omdia recognition is earned — but only creates value when actively used. Exotel currently has five active recognitions. None of them are compounding in market yet.

B. Who is stealing business.

Five movements reshaping the India CX + CPaaS market around Exotel.

01

AI-voice-native startups on the rise

Bolna, Gnani, SquadStack, Vomyra, DialNexa, FreJun — TRAI-compliant, +91 numbers, Hinglish tuned. They are eating mid-market AI voice deals Exotel used to close by default.

02

US voice-AI stack entering via partners

Vapi, Retell, Bland, ElevenLabs increasingly deployed via India partners. They fail on India latency and Hinglish — but narrative pull with CTOs is real.

03

The voice layer is going down to infra

ElevenLabs powers Cars24, Razorpay, Unacademy, Meesho, Apna (7.5M AI interview minutes). Infra-tier players are claiming the "voice quality" premium.

04

Ozonetel is closing the mid-market

7× G2 review authority. Monthly category listicles. 4.6 / 5 rating vs Exotel 4.4. For every "best cloud telephony India" query, Ozonetel shows up with a fresh page. Default recommendation in AI answers.

05

CX trend: orchestration > products

Frost Radar 2025, IDC 2025, Omdia all flag the same shift: CPaaS + CCaaS + UCaaS converging into AI orchestration layers. This is where Harmony sits — and where a unified intelligence layer is the only way to hold the narrative.
