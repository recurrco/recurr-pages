§01 The headline finding

One sentence

Exotel wins the two queries buyers used in 2022 and is invisible on the four queries buyers are asking today. The gap is not a 6–8 month forecast — it is the current state of India's SERP and LLM answer layer, and it compounds every week until a distribution engine is pointed at it.

Three numbers that anchor the rest of this page:

  • 6.5× — Ozonetel's LLM-citation growth rate vs Exotel (+222% YoY vs +34% YoY).
  • -1,100 — Referring main domains Exotel has lost over the last 12 months.
  • 7× — Ozonetel's G2 review volume vs Exotel (623 vs 89). The moat behind who LLMs quote.

The 6-month problem

Ozonetel grew from 9 LLM queries/mo (Apr 2025) to 29/mo (Mar 2026). That's a 3.2× jump in 12 months. Exotel grew from 29 to 39 — a 1.34× jump. If both curves extrapolate forward six months, Ozonetel overtakes Exotel on LLM citation volume and becomes the default AI answer for "best cloud telephony India".

Inverting that trajectory requires a concentrated backlink + G2 + listicle-authoring push starting in Q1. Not Q3.

Reading this

Raw backlink rank puts Exotel middle-of-pack — ahead of Ozonetel and Knowlarity. But rank alone misses the picture: what kind of backlinks, from where, and in which direction.

Exotel has 22,419 backlinks vs Ozonetel's 4,292 — a 5× raw advantage. But only 920 of Exotel's referring links are India-origin (vs Ozonetel's 1,205). And only 1,645 (8%) of Exotel's links are in article-context locations — the kind LLMs actually use to construct answers. The rest sit in footers, nav menus, and a .nl automated network that carries little weight.

Why this matters

When ChatGPT or Perplexity constructs an answer to "best cloud telephony India", it heavily weights third-party review sites. Ozonetel's 623 reviews give it 7× the corroboration Exotel core has — and 2.5× more than Ameyo-by-Exotel. Even combining Exotel + Ameyo (89 + 251 = 340), Ozonetel still leads 1.83×.

Closing this gap is not a content play. It's a systematic G2 review solicitation campaign across 12 months, tied to onboarding, support moments, and CSM motion. The Hub module tracks this weekly.

§09 What the data adds up to

Five compounding reads from everything above.

Read 01 · The search surface is splitting

Google SERP still favours Exotel on legacy terms ("cloud telephony", "IVR for business"). LLM answer layer already favours Ozonetel / Bolna / Gnani / prospeo. Buyer behaviour is migrating from the former to the latter. Exotel's legacy strength does not cover the new surface.

Read 02 · Ozonetel's compounding is the immediate threat

+222% YoY LLM citations. 623 G2 reviews. 8+ category listicles. Publishing monthly. This is a compounding motion, and Exotel has nothing matching it. Every month Exotel does not match the cadence, the gap widens by approximately 5–7%.

Read 03 · Exotel's content is airborne, its authority is on the ground

20+ Q1 2026 pillar posts — the strongest AI-Led-CX thesis on any India CPaaS site. None of them are indexed, linked, or cited yet. "Why US voice AI breaks in India" (March 31) is 18 days old and has zero backlinks. Content quality is ~9/10. Distribution surface is ~2/10.

Read 04 · The IDC Leader placement is undervalued

Exotel earned IDC MarketScape Leader (Nov 25) + Frost Radar (Dec 26) + Omdia next-gen CPaaS (Dec 30) in a five-week window. Individually, each of these is a 6-month LinkedIn + PR + sales-enablement campaign. Collectively, they were announced and then left to sit. This is the highest-leverage unused asset on the table.

Read 05 · The window closes in 6–8 months

Search surfaces calcify. Once LLMs repeatedly cite Ozonetel for "best cloud telephony India", they learn the citation as ground truth and reinforce it. Overturning a calcified LLM citation takes 3–5× the effort of winning it while the surface is still fluid. The window is now.
