03 — FY25-26 Retrospective

What we built.

What worked

  • Infrastructure. Claude Code, MCP servers, Outwork for project management, full workspace architecture.
  • recurr.co. 7 pages live on V5 design system. Brand identity defined.
  • Press architecture. S3 vision, editorial architecture, 175 articles extracted and ready for audit.
  • IGS framework. 6 layers defined, stack philosophy set.
  • Service packaging. Positioning and content — two capabilities, clearly defined.
  • First design partnership approved. Intelligence sprint in progress.

What didn't

  • Consistent revenue. Client work was sporadic. Engagements started and dropped without follow-on.
  • Consistent publishing. Press has been "about to launch" for months. Zero published content.
  • Sharp GTM. No clear pipeline. No productized offering. Leads came from network, not inbound.
  • IGS deployment. Framework defined but not built. No property has all 6 layers working.
03 — Continued

The honest
assessment.

We rebuilt the foundation but didn't generate consistent revenue or publish consistently. The infrastructure is ready. The positioning is clear. Now we execute.

At its peak, Recurr was doing ₹2.5 Cr in revenue. It dropped. FY25-26 was a rebuild year. Year 3 is about compounding on that foundation.

If Press works, everything else follows.

Press builds authority. Authority drives inbound. Inbound feeds client services. This is the flywheel.

Press is not one person writing alone — it's Recurr convening the B2B conversation. Guest posts, co-authored pieces, interviews, podcast. AI-embedded production with human editorial judgment.

Six editorial pillars

  • Positioning & Category Design
  • GTM & Go-to-Market
  • Content & Distribution

  • AI & the New Stack

06 — Principles

How we operate.

Practice what we preach

Press is Client Zero. If our own content engine doesn't work, we have no business selling it to others.

Productise relentlessly

Onboarding is intensive and custom. Everything after that is systemised — playbooks, templates, the portal, IGS checklists.

Inbound over outbound

By Q4, 80%+ of leads come from Press + recurr.co + LinkedIn. Not from the founder's personal network.

Every rupee has a thesis

No spend without a clear expected outcome. Always know what success looks like before committing capital.

07 — Risks

What could go wrong.

Press doesn't publish

The flywheel never starts. No authority, no inbound.

Sacrosanct commitment. Minimum 2/week. AI-embedded production. April 3 is day one.

Pipeline doesn't fill

Revenue stays dependent on one design partnership.

Network push in Q1. LinkedIn activation. Don't wait for inbound — earn it.

Can't scale past 5 clients

Revenue caps at ₹15-20L/month.

Productise harder. Account management hires. IGS makes each deployment faster.

Creative bench stretched

Ventures + clients + Press competing for same designers.

Client work pays, so it gets priority. Ventures get scheduled time. Hire more if revenue supports it.

Media monetisation slow

₹65L from sponsorships doesn't materialise year one.

Start conversations Q2. Lower target to ₹30-40L. Audience first, monetisation follows.
