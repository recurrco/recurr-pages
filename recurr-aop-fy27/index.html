03C — Vayam

Vayam.

Recurr has worked with B2B software companies and consumer brands. We have never seriously ventured into governance, social impact, and the public sector in India — despite planning to for years. Vayam changes that.

Vayam is a media vertical covering the intersection of economy, policy, and frontier technology — minerals, mining, defence, energy, critical infrastructure. The economic layers that power the country but are barely covered. These are sectors with few players, all capital-rich, all in need of communication design, and all driven by thought leadership and media relationships.

Functionally, Vayam builds press and media relations capability — newspapers, television, policy publications. The kind of media strength that Recurr has never had to build for software clients. This is where we learn to operate at that level.

As we cross five years and qualify for government procurement (GeM), Vayam represents the foundation of an entire B2G and public sector vertical. The first project (Anthracite — a book on India's mineral sovereignty) is in production. Zero revenue expectations in Year 1. This is a five-year bet.
