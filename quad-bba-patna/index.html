FAQ

Questions students & parents ask

Who awards the degree? Is it recognised?
Quad delivers the industry-led programme and skilling; your degree is awarded by our partner university. Quad AI (Onenorth Learning Pvt. Ltd.) is a training institute, not a UGC-regulated HEI — so you get a recognised degree and a portfolio of real business work.
Why Quad over a regular BBA or a metro B-school?
You get IIM-grade mentorship and a build-real-businesses classroom — without relocating, and at ~₹4L all-in for three years vs. lakhs a year at a metro. You graduate with proof of work (a portfolio + a launched project), not just marks.
What placement support do I actually get?
Dedicated 1:1 career coaching, resume reviews and mock interviews, an 8–10 week industry immersion / live consulting project, and access to a 75+ company hiring network. We’re early — so we’re transparent: we sell support and access, not a placement statistic.
What does the ₹4L cover, and is EMI available?
It’s all-in for the full three years — tuition, exams, software and experiential learning. Scholarships up to 50% are available, and EMI / education loans through our finance partners.
Can I specialise? In what?
Yes — in your final year you choose one of four tracks: Marketing & Growth, Finance & Consulting, Entrepreneurship & Startups, or Business Analytics & AI Strategy.
What is the QAT, and how hard is it?
A 60-minute online aptitude test covering logical reasoning, data interpretation, quantitative ability and communication. It’s built to find potential and fit — not to trip you up.
