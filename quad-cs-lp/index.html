FAQ

Questions students & parents ask

Who awards the degree? Is it recognised?
Quad delivers the industry-led program and skilling; your degree is awarded by our partner university. Quad AI (Onenorth Learning Pvt. Ltd.) is a training institute, not a UGC-regulated Higher Education Institution — so you get an industry-ready skillset and a recognised degree from the partnering university.
What placement support do I actually get?
Dedicated 1:1 career coaching, resume reviews and mock interviews with MAANG engineers, a mandatory 6-month paid internship, and access to a 75+ company hiring network. (We’re early — so we’re transparent: we sell support and access, not a placement statistic.)
How do the scholarships work?
Scholarships range from 10%–50% of tuition across four programs — Women in Tech, Early-Mover, Udaan (top-percentile QAT scorers) and JP Narayan (changemakers, via interview). Your counsellor helps you find the one you qualify for.
What does the fee cover, and is EMI available?
Fees are all-in — tuition, software, development and experiential learning. 4-year totals are ₹8L (Patna), ₹9.5L (Mangalore) and ₹14L (Bengaluru). EMI and education loans are available through our finance partners.
What is the QAT, and how hard is it?
The QAT is a 60-minute online aptitude test covering logical reasoning, data interpretation, quantitative ability and communication. It’s designed to find fit and potential — not to trip you up.
Which campuses can I join?
Bengaluru, Mangalore and Patna are live for the 2026 batch. Mumbai is coming soon.
