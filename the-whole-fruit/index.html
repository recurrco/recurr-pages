Why We Exist

India hasn't really innovated on fruits.

Think about it. What exists today? Juice bars using overripe stock. Smoothies diluted with water and sugar. Cut fruit platters that look sad by the time they arrive. Fruit salads drowning in cream.

The category is broken. And nobody seems to care.

Smoothie bowls are the best thing you can do with fruits today — and almost nobody in India is doing them well. We changed that. Fresh stock. No sugar added. No water added.
