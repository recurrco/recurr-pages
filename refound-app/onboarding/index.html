Before there's an Inbox or a single number, the engine has to be told who the buyer is, what they search, and where to look. Onboarding is the act of setting all of that — most of it our work, two things we need from Exotel. When the inputs are in, one run turns them into a live rabbithole and Exotel goes Active.
