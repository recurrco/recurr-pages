Rules

The Non-Negotiables.

1. Caricatures are the brand. They go everywhere. No MOO asset without a character.
2. Bebas Neue for all headlines. All caps. No thin fonts. No serif. Ever.
3. Brand palette ≠ category palette. Don't use Royal Blue as a category.
4. Grid pattern on Blue and Dark surfaces only. Never on Oat Beige.
5. Yellow = primary energy. Red = action/CTA. Blue = background. Oat = editorial.
6. Interactions bounce. 120ms. cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1). Everything alive.
7. Angled boxes, starbursts, sketch circles = MOO's playful toolkit. Use them.
8. Food photography > caricature illustrations > text. Food is the hero.
9. Every copy passes the "Never Be Boring" filter. If it's boring, rewrite it.
10. Logo must appear on every page. White version on dark/blue. Black version on oat/white.
