Content · Y1 priority14 / 17

Year-1 focus: Patna & Mangalore.

Of the ten streams, we run hard on two first. Low competition, real demand, supply-side gap on organic search. These are the campuses where we win fast — and the proof point for the access claim.

Y1 Priority · Edge

Patna

Eastern India's largest urban catchment with almost no premium CS-AI brand presence. Organic rankings come fast. Counsellor + offline channels relatively open.

Y1 Priority · Edge

Mangalore

Tier-2 South India catchment with limited new-age tech-school competition. Same supply-side gap. Same fast-win opportunity.

Why not lead with Bengaluru?   The Bangalore campus becomes the anchor in steady state — but it competes with BITS, Christ, RV, Manipal on home turf. Faster to plant the flag where competition is thin, then let downstream brand juice flow into Bengaluru in Year 2.
Content · Stream cadence15 / 17

Cadence per stream.

Six-week first cycle. We run it on the Patna and Mangalore streams first — those are the two we're picking to ship fastest. Once the cadence productises, the other streams plug in.

Week 1
Intel Stream intelligence + calendar. Keyword mapping, SERP audit, AEO surfaces, off-site thread inventory. Output: a per-stream work list.
Week 2
Edit Editorial work begins. Briefs written for Patna and Mangalore streams. Drafts in motion.
Week 3
Ship One new link on the website every day. Page, listicle entry, comparison, or guide. Daily cadence locks the engine.
Week 4 – 6
Repeat Productise the cycle. Templates, briefs library, editor workflow. Same cadence runs on autopilot. Other streams begin plugging in.
