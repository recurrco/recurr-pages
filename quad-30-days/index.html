Positioning · Thesis03 / 15

Bottom-up. Product first, then promise.

Quad sells a finite catalogue of {Campus × Course} products. The persona is fixed — Class 12 student and the parent paying the fee. Everything we say has to sell one of those specific products to that specific buyer.

The Products

Campus × Course permutations.

Patna · Mangalore · Bengaluru · Mumbai. Each campus stocks a subset of the catalogue: 4-yr UG CS-AI/ML, UG/PG Management, BCA, and adjacencies.

Roughly 9–12 active combinations in steady state. The website's IA, the ad copy, and the SEO map all derive from this matrix.

The Buyer

Tier-2/3 parent, IIT ambition unmet.

Kid scored well but didn't land an IIT/NIT seat. Family has ₹8L available, wants a credentialled CS-AI program with industry-grade outcomes and a brand they can name at weddings.

Demographics shift across campuses; the underlying job-to-be-done doesn't.

Hub-and-spoke call

In 12 months, the brand reads Bangalore-anchored, nationally distributed — Manipal / Amity / ISB precedent. Patna and Mangalore are the real edge: lower competition, faster organic wins, and the proof point for the access claim.

Channel mix · Benchmarks08 / 15

What's the current admission season telling us?

Before deciding what to scale or kill, we have to read the scorecard. Sujit pulls together the current-state benchmark across paid, organic, and referral. Three questions we want this slide to answer next week.

Q1
What's actually working?
Cost per qualified lead by channel. Conversion to admission, not to enquiry. Tier-2/3 vs Tier-1 split.
Q2
Where are we leaking?
Enquiry → counsellor → admission funnel. Phone DB hygiene. Time-to-first-touch. Drop-off geographies.
Q3
What's missing?
Organic search. AEO presence on Quora and ChatGPT. Listicle and review-site coverage. Referral capture.
Pending from Sujit

Channel-level spend and lead numbers, current paid mix, GA / GSC / Meta Ads access status, counsellor CRM export. Bring to next working session.

Channel mix · Year-1 inputs09 / 15

Where we're already directionally clear.

Three calls we can make even before the benchmark lands. Two unlocks, one gap.

01
Google Ads — scale independently.
Works for the current scenario because it doesn't depend on the website. We can spin independent landing pages and drive paid traffic in parallel with the rebuild.
02
GA / GTM — stitch immediately.
Critical, blocking everything else. Without instrumentation we're scaling spend into a black box. Goes into the W1 Sanity build.
!
Phone database — gap.
Currently unplanned. During admission season, this is where the money sits. We need to know what happens to a captured number today before we add more spend.
Adjacencies we're not yet committing to

Meta / Instagram for awareness, YouTube long-form, counsellor incentive programs, regional-language paid, school partnerships. Each gets a yes/no decision after the W1 benchmark.

Two axes cut everything: audience and campus. Audiences split four ways, campuses split into a hub-and-spoke. Every piece of content earns its keep on at least one cell.

The actual user of the product. Wants identity, peer signal, future-proof skills.

Writes the cheque. Wants credibility, outcomes, ROI, family-name legibility.

Content · Off-website15 / 15

Off-website — starts streaming next week.

The fastest content surface. No website dependency, no design queue. Intelligence first (where the high-value threads live), then daily human engagement.

Surface 01

Reddit

r/india, r/bangalore, r/JEEneetards, r/indianstudents. Honest answers in admission-season threads. Brand-safe, not promotional.

Surface 02

Quora

Long-tail admission questions with permanent SEO value. AEO surface for Class 12 + parent queries.

Surface 03

YouTube comments

High-traffic admission and CS-AI explainer videos. Engagement, not spam — adds value to the thread first.

Surface 04

Listicles & review sites

Shiksha, CollegeDekho, CollegeDunia, BookMyCollege. Listing presence, profile completeness, review hygiene.

First-week operating model
Mon → Wed

Intelligence.

Thread inventory across Reddit, Quora, YouTube. Score by traffic × buyer-relevance. Output: a Top-50 weekly engagement list.

Thu → Sun

Engagement.

Daily comment / answer cadence. Recurr writes the first 10, then hand-off to Quad team with a written voice guide.
