Tiffins are free. That's the point.
Tiffins is the Lunch Drop play. Free daily meals platform for offices. We make money on commission, not subscription. The funnel is flipped — free tiffins pull offices in, paid modules keep them.
Step 1
Free tiffins
Office signs up. Employees get daily meals. Zero cost to the company.

→
Step 3

→
Step 4
Volume & scale
More offices = more volume. 5% commission on all tiffin orders. Everyone earns.
