Fold 01 · why

Headline sells an outcome, not a catalog title. Live page led with “UG programme in Computer Science (AI & ML)” — a name, not a reason. We lead with the single most credible differentiator (real MAANG mentors) + concrete proof in the subhead.

Form is inline and above the fold — phone + city only. The decision moment and the ask are on the same screen; no “tap Apply → modal” friction.

Killed: full nav hamburger, the “Brochure” escape-hatch button, the 4 logistics cards (4 Years / 120 Seats / Full-time / In-Campus), and the floating WhatsApp button that overlapped the CTA. Logistics move to Fold 02 as proof.

Fold 08 · why

Fee, scholarship and loans are fused into one “Investment” block. On the live page the fee table sat alone (sticker shock), with scholarships and loan partners as separate sections further down. Here the objection and its three answers live on one screen.

Transparent per-city pricing is a genuine differentiator in Indian edu — we keep it, but pair it instantly with “here’s how you afford it,” and turn the CTA into a value-add (“check eligibility”) rather than a raw ask.

Live page had only 4 FAQs. The most important one — “who awards the degree?” — was hidden in a footer regulatory disclaimer (Quad is a training institute, degrees come from a partner university, not a UGC HEI). Answering that openly defuses the biggest trust objection instead of letting a prospect find it in the fine print.

Placement FAQ stays honest: support + paid internship + hiring network. No invented %.
