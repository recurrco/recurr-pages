UX Principles

Five rules we never break.

💤

Invisible Until Delightful

The system runs on autopilot. You barely interact. But when you do, it's a tiny moment of joy.

🪄

Zero Onboarding

Employee gets a WhatsApp link, taps it, and they're already subscribed. Everything pre-filled by admin. Magic.

🤝

Trust the Defaults

Defaults are so good, most users never change them. Menu auto-decided. Subscription always on. Zero config.

📱

WhatsApp-First, App-Second

The daily relationship lives on WhatsApp. The app is for editing, exploring, and configuring. Not for daily use.

😍

Emoji Reactions Over Forms

One-tap emoji feedback (😍 😊 😐 😞) per dish. Fun, low friction, generates useful data. No star ratings, no surveys.
