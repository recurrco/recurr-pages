The Sector

Why this desk exists, written in plain terms.

India will not be a passive consumer of foreign AI for the next twenty years. That single sentence is what the country has decided. It is not a debate inside Sanchar Bhavan or Niti Aayog. The debate is over. The question now is what sovereignty looks like in practice — across compute, chips, data, models, talent, governance, and trust.

This desk covers all seven layers of that bet. The Sovereign Compute Mission and its seven sites. The fourteen labs trying to ship a competitive Indian foundation model. The two semiconductor fabs being built in Gujarat. The Indic-language data that has to be assembled before the model can read it. The talent flow from the US back to Bangalore that started in 2023. The policy stack — the DPDP Act, the AI Mission, the proposed Sovereign AI Bill of 2026.

We cover all of it. From inside the buildings. With photographs and renders that are ours, charts that are sourced, and conversations on the record where we can get them and off the record where we cannot.
