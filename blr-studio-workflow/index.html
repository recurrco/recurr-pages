How it hangs together

Page-first: research once, cut everywhere.
The spine · Sanity

One source becomes many. The master gets milked.

Every topic becomes one fully-researched, well-made master — a page + an anchor video. Every other format is a cut of that master, not fresh work. Sanity holds the queue, the state, and the structured content; Creative produces into it, Growth programmes out of it, and the numbers loop back to shape what we make next.

04

From here

How we start moving — and build the habit.
1

The first move — week zero (3–5 days)

Two things at once. Stand up the spine + tools (Sanity queue live, everyone on their pod's stack). And a tool sprint — get functional, not expert: each person drives their core tool once on a throwaway. In parallel, Programming drafts week one's slate. Gate by Friday: everyone can drive their tools, and next Monday's calendar is loaded. No longer than that.

2

The weekly rhythm

Mon
Plan & brief

Programming locks the week + briefs Creative. Both pods align.

Tue–Thu
Make + move

Creative produces; Growth publishes to calendar + engages.

1–2×/wk
Shoot days

Batch a week+ of real footage in one session.

Fri
Review + plan-ahead

Read the numbers; brief next week; push the horizon out.

3

The daily beat

Async morning check-in on Slack (~10 min): what's shipping, what's blocked. Growth publishes + engages + watches the numbers. Creative works the queue for upcoming slots. End of day: one line — shipped / next.

4

The horizon ladder — the habit we're building

Plan further out, every week
Week 1: 1 week ahead  →  Week 2: 2 weeks  →  Weeks 3–4: a rolling 30-day calendar
The one rule: never end a week without next week locked — and the horizon pushed one notch further. That's how "plan Monday's week" quietly becomes "always a month ahead."
