04

Client & discipline

Client touchpoints

MonthlySign off the strategy + calendar.
WeeklyQuick check-in, approvals, performance readout.
Ad-hocLaunches & offers fed through Client Relationships only — one throat.

Two non-negotiables

One calendar. Plan & brief in one place; schedule & publish in the scheduler. Never three calendars.

One channel to the client. All inputs and approvals route through Client Relationships, so the bench stays heads-down on production.
