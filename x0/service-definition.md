## Positioning

**One-liner:** We build the 0→1 of design for B2B software businesses.

**Category:** B2B design studio — first versions only.

**Core thesis:** Every B2B company reaches a moment where they need to look serious for the first time. Their first real brand. Their first real website. Their first real product design. That's the moment x0 exists for. We are the first serious design investment you make into your company.

**The catch:** We only do first versions. No rebrands. No v2s. Rebrands and redesigns carry baggage — legacy decisions, internal politics, accumulated compromises. That's not what excites us. We want the blank canvas. The 0→1.

**What it replaces:** Freelance designers who don't understand B2B, agencies with too much overhead, or the founder doing it themselves in Canva.

---

## Philosophy

### Why 0→1 Only
- First versions are pure. No baggage, no legacy, no "but we've always done it this way."
- The constraints are clearer: you know what you're building, you just need someone who can make it look and feel right.
- We're building first impressions — the first time the world sees your product, your website, your brand. That's the most exciting design problem.
- Rebrands require managing internal stakeholders, legacy systems, and emotional attachment. That's consulting, not design.

### B2B Design Expertise
- We know what designing for business buyers means. Enterprise buyers don't browse — they evaluate. They don't impulse-buy — they build business cases. The design has to work differently.
- B2B design is not "boring corporate." It's design that respects the buyer's intelligence, communicates value clearly, and converts through trust, not tricks.
- We understand the B2B buying journey: discovery → evaluation → demo → procurement. Every screen we design maps to a moment in that journey.

### Research & Publishing
We're curious about what great B2B design looks like. So we research and share openly:
- Design teardowns of products doing it well
- "This vs That" redesign comparisons
- "Why this converts" breakdowns
- AI crawler / SEO structure explainers
- Build-in-public posts from real client projects
- Hot takes on design trends in SaaS

This isn't a content marketing play — it's how we think. Publishing forces clarity.

## Delivery Model

Sprint-based. Every engagement is a defined sprint with clear deliverables, timeline, and price. No hourly billing, no scope creep, no retainer lock-in.

1. **Kickoff call** — understand the product, audience, and goals
2. **Sprint** — design work delivered in the defined timeline
3. **Review & iteration** — built-in rounds of feedback
4. **Handoff** — final files, assets, code (where applicable), and documentation
5. **Post-launch support** — 30 days for website projects
