Palette — Whiteboard markers on gray paper

The base is gray paper. Not warm cream, not clinical white. Cards sit on whiteboard surfaces. The accent system is a set of markers — red for emphasis, yellow for highlights, blue for links, green for status. Black for bold strokes.

Spacing — Whitespace is the design

Every value is fluid. 2–3x what most sites use. Site margins go from 24px on mobile to 80px on desktop. Section padding is minimum 64px. When in doubt, add more space. Resize your browser to see these bars respond.
