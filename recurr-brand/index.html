The Whiteboard Layer — Where tech meets thinking

ASCII diagrams assemble character-by-character on scroll. Pure JS, no libraries, under 2KB. The subtle technology layer you feel but can't name. That's craft.

Scroll-triggered. ~400 characters. 3ms per character. Total draw time: ~1.2 seconds.
