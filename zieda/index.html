April 2030 · Feature

            
Zieda’s Opens First Store in Singapore

            
The Dubai-born beauty group becomes the first GCC aesthetic brand to enter Southeast Asia. A four-floor flagship combining salon, clinic, and Zieda’s own cosmetics line. The opening marks the completion of a decade-long journey from a single villa on Jumeirah Beach Road to a pan-regional category leader.

          


          

            
April 2029 · Profile

            
Zieda’s Launches Own Cosmetics

            
A premium range built on a decade of clinical experience. Professionals first; consumer launch to follow. The line is formulated with Russian dermatology principles and tested across Zieda’s own clinical network — a distribution model the group describes as “earned before it’s sold.”

          


          

            
April 2027 · Launch

            
Zieda’s Opens Beauty Academy

            
Training and consulting for the GCC’s beauty industry, led by founder Zieda Sharipova. The academy will serve practitioners, salon owners, and distributors from Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and the UAE — with a stated ambition to set regional standards for aesthetic practice.
