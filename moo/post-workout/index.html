Oatmeal Shakes (4)

Rolled oats cooked down, then blended with milk or oatmilk, flavour, and your protein. Dense, drinkable, and calorie-loaded. The cooked-oat carbs have a higher glycaemic index than whole oats — which is exactly what you want after a lift, because insulin spikes shuttle glucose and amino acids into tired muscle. Milk adds both casein (slow) and whey (fast) for a full 3–4 hour MPS window. Built for heavy training days, two-a-days, and anyone chasing a surplus.
