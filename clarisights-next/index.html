What's changing

Performance marketing teams sit at the most complicated seat in their company. New channels — retail media, connected TV, podcast, in-game, influencer — arrive faster than the last generation of tooling can absorb them. Measurement has moved from deterministic clicks to modeled conversions and probabilistic attribution. App and web split into ecosystems that don't talk to each other. Media mix models and incrementality tests moved from academic exercises to standard practice. And AI arrived as both a new surface in search results and the interface teams increasingly want to use to interact with their own data.

Reporting is no longer an output. It's the surface where decisions happen, where workflows live, where your AI works alongside you. The questions your team asks — how long until this cohort pays back, is paid cannibalizing organic, how does our brand appear in an LLM-generated result — cut across sources, need flexible modeling, and increasingly must be answered inside whatever tool the team is already in.

The new Clarisights is that surface. New architecture. New data model. New interface. A completely new product, built for the marketing landscape of tomorrow.

Principle 01

Any data in. Any data out.

Your marketing data in one shape. Then available everywhere.

The new data model unifies paid, organic, app, web, competitive, AI search, product catalog, and affiliates in a single flexible layer — built not just for today's channels, but for whatever comes next.

Inside Clarisights, that model powers LTV curves, payback periods, cohort analysis, modeled revenue, and variable payouts natively.

Outside Clarisights, it becomes the cleanest source of marketing data your measurement team has ever had: pre-harmonized, versioned, incrementally updated, ready to feed a media mix model or an incrementality test without anyone building a pipeline from scratch.

Data flows in from everywhere. Intelligence flows out to everyone who needs it.

Principle 03

Built for humans and machines.

Reports are documents now. Collaboration is inline. Your AI is a first-class user.

Most software treats AI as an afterthought — a chat window pasted onto an interface designed for humans only. The new Clarisights treats both as first-class users.

For practitioners: reports work like documents, not dashboards. A blank canvas where you add tables, charts, annotations, and text on a single surface. Collaboration happens inline, next to the data it's about. Clean and immediate for a new user. Infinitely flexible for a power user who needs custom cohort windows and modeled coefficients.

For machines: the data model, report structure, and platform surface are all designed so an LLM can parse your data, build reports, and make changes inside Clarisights as naturally as a human can. Through the Model Context Protocol, your harmonized cross-channel metrics are accessible from Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini — or whatever comes next.

Build a report on Monday. Ask your AI tool to extend it on Tuesday.
