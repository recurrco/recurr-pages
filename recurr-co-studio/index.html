About

Smart people with real skills, trapped in freelance mode.

That's the pattern Recurr was built to solve. We find experienced operators who are great at what they do but can't scale beyond personal bandwidth — because scaling a service business requires brand, pipeline, billing, and a system around you.

Recurr is that system. Each product in the studio is led by someone who's done the work for years. We give them the infrastructure to turn expertise into a real business. Every product has its own service model and a software layer that makes the experience meaningfully better.

We're not building the next SaaS unicorn. We're building a portfolio of focused, profitable service businesses that are better because they have technology in the right places.
