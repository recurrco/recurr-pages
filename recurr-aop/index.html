Key Principles

01 "Every rupee has a thesis." No spend without a clear hypothesis. Test, measure, iterate.
02 "Practice what we preach." Press = Client Zero. We deploy IGS on ourselves before selling it.
03 "Productize relentlessly." Onboarding is custom. Everything after that is systemized.
04 "Inbound over outbound." 80%+ leads from Press + recurr.co + LinkedIn by Q4.
12

Risks

Press doesn't publish consistently
Editorial calendar locked. AI-assisted production. Min 2/week non-negotiable. Accountability via Outwork tracking.
Pipeline doesn't fill
Network-first outreach Q1. Press builds inbound by Q2-Q3. If lagging, activate paid LinkedIn + cold outbound.
Can't scale past 5 clients
Productized retainers reduce per-client time. Sujith + interns handle execution. Pratik focuses on positioning only.
Sujith can't own client execution
Invest in onboarding + frameworks. Co-serve first 2-3 clients. Graduate to solo by Q2. Hire backup if needed.
Media monetization is slow
Media revenue is gravy, not the meal. Client services is the core engine. Keep media costs near zero.
Outwork bench stretched thin
Prioritize client work > Press > ventures. Hire freelancers for peak load. Say no to scope creep.
