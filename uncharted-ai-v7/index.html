The Technology

The Exploration Platform for our planet. And beyond.

Four integrated modules that take you from satellite imagery to subsurface intelligence — fully autonomous, with 150x the data density of manual surveys.

Satellite Terrain Scan Contour Map Visualization
01
Autonomous Ground Navigation
TRACE

Tracked ground robots that navigate extreme, GPS-denied terrain autonomously. SLAM-based navigation, swarm coordination, and decentralized communications let our robots go where humans can't — and stay there for extended missions.

LiDAR Point Cloud Elevation Heatmap
02
Subsurface Data Acquisition
Multi-Modal Sensing

LiDAR, hyperspectral imaging, X-ray fluorescence, and ground-penetrating radar working in concert. Contact-based measurements that only a ground robot can perform — capturing the data that satellites and drones cannot see.

Traversability Map Segmented Terrain Analysis
03
AI-Guided Exploration Intelligence
FiND

ML-guided planning, perception, and real-time decision-making. FiND (Field Intelligence for Navigation and Discovery) fuses multi-modal sensor data into actionable intelligence — determining where to go next, what to measure, and why.

3D Subsurface Model Risk Map Wireframe
04
Real-Time 3D Subsurface Modeling
Digital Twin

A living 3D subsurface model built in real-time as robots traverse terrain. All sensor data fused into one explorable map. Where to drill is no longer a guess — it's a data-driven decision backed by multi-modal evidence.
