FAQ

Questions students & parents ask

Is the degree recognised? Who awards it?
Yes — your BCA degree is awarded by Mangalore University through our partner Trisha College. Quad adds the industry-grade AI skilling, mentorship and placement support on top of the recognised university degree.
Why Quad’s BCA over a traditional BCA?
Same recognised degree — but you’re taught by working MAANG engineers, you write 1,00,000+ lines of real code, you specialise in AI/Web/BI/Cybersecurity, and you get a 75+ company hiring network instead of outdated theory.
What placement support do I actually get?
Dedicated 1:1 career coaching, resume reviews and mock interviews with MAANG engineers, internships, and access to a 75+ company hiring network. We’re early — so we’re transparent: we sell support and access, not a placement statistic.
What does the ₹3.6L cover, and is EMI available?
It’s all-in for the full three years — tuition, exams, software and experiential learning. Scholarships up to 50% are available, and EMI / education loans through our finance partners.
Can I specialise? In what?
Yes — in your final year you choose one of four tracks: Web Development, AI/ML Applications, Business Intelligence, or Cybersecurity.
What is the QAT, and how hard is it?
A 60-minute online aptitude test covering logical reasoning, data interpretation, quantitative ability and communication. It’s built to find potential and fit — not to trip you up.
