Hunt / 01 Findability

Be findable everywhere your buyer looks.

Your buyer's journey no longer runs through one channel. Publish for Google, AI answer engines, Reddit, directories, and the long tail of surfaces where discovery actually happens today. Visibility isn't a single surface anymore — it's every surface.

Hunt / 02 Recall

Be the first brand your buyer remembers.

Own the topics that matter in your category. Build a body of writing, research, and conversation that keeps you top-of-mind before buyers ever open a comparison tab — whether that buyer is a CFO with a budget or a consumer with a cart.

Hunt / 03 Decision

Win the decision, not just the click.

When your buyer is evaluating, they need evidence — not awareness. Landing pages, case studies, competitive displacement, and bottom-funnel content that move them from short-list to signature, from basket to checkout.
