A 60% repeat rate. Who — and why?
60%repeat rate

Who repeats

  • Health-conscious urban Bengaluru, 25–40, ordering breakfast & everyday meals
  • Habit buyers, not occasion buyers — breakfast is a daily ritual
  • Skews repeat over reach: a loyal base, not one-time trials

Why they come back

  • Genuinely healthy and genuinely tasty — rare to get both
  • Daily-occasion product — breakfast repeats by nature
  • Ever-changing menu — new oat categories keep it fresh
  • Fast + reliable — ~6-min kitchen, consistent quality
