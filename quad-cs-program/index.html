FAQs

Questions parents & students ask

Is this a recognised degree?
Yes. The degree is awarded by Quad's partner university; Quad delivers the market-led, industry curriculum, faculty network, projects and placement support around it. You graduate with a recognised university degree and a job-ready portfolio. (Full regulatory note is in the footer.)
What does "100% placement assistance" actually mean?
Every student gets dedicated, structured support: a paid 6-month internship, a 1:1 career coach, resume building, interview preparation, and direct introductions to 75+ marquee recruiters. It is committed support to make you placement-ready — not a guaranteed-salary promise.
How much does it cost, and are scholarships available?
Total four-year fees are ₹8L (Patna), ₹9.5L (Mangalore) or ₹14L (Bengaluru). Scholarships range from 10% to 50% of tuition based on merit, plus dedicated programs for women in tech and early applicants. We also partner with six education-loan providers.
Who actually teaches the program?
Curriculum is co-curated by IIT & IIM alumni and delivered by 100+ mentors actively working at companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Apple, CRED and Salesforce. 75% of faculty are CXOs and founders flying into campus to teach.
What's the difference between the three campuses?
Same curriculum, same faculty network, same placement support across Bengaluru, Mangalore and Patna. The main differences are location and fee structure. You pick your preferred campus during the application.
What are the eligibility criteria?
Class X and XII completion (expected or final) from any recognised board, plus the QAT aptitude test and a 1:1 interview. There's no application fee.
