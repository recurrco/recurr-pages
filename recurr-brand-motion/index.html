Brief

Motion & ASCII as structural, not decorative. The page breathes — numbers tick, rules reveal, diagrams flash, loaders wait. Six categories: motion primitives (atmosphere, instrumentation, reveal) and ASCII graphics (diagrams, charts, loaders).

Notes

Each specimen is isolated, reusable, dependency-free (vanilla JS / Canvas / CSS). ASCII specimens are plain <pre> blocks — paste into case studies, diagrams, pitch decks. Motion always serves the content: atmosphere sits behind, instrumentation runs in the margin, reveals pace the read, loaders explain the wait.
