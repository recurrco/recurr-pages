Section / 03 — Studios Four industry theses

What we're building.

We organise our work by industry, not by ownership. Each studio runs client engagements and our own ventures under the same thesis — because the work it takes to win a category is the same whether the cap table is ours or somebody else's.

Studio / 01 — Enterprise Tech Practice lead · Sujith

The decade of decision software.

Enterprise teams have spent ten years building reporting tools and getting worse at decisions. The next stack — decision software, agentic workflows, internal AI — is starting to ship. We work with the firms building it, and we are building inside it ourselves.

A media house — and a stack — for the country that builds.

Heavy industry, frontier-sector startups, the institutions inside India's tectonic foundation. The firms here rarely get covered with the depth they deserve, and the software they need is barely being built. We publish about them, and we build for them.

Four years inside a cloud kitchen brand changes how you see this industry. We know what works, what doesn't, and what the next decade of Indian F&B — DTC, embedded, retail, subscription — looks like from inside the door. We are building both a brand and a service for it.
