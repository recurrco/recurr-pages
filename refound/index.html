The Internet Changed

Every brand wants to be found by their customer. The biggest channels on the planet are still search engines — and now a growing wave of LLMs. The other big channel is user-generated content: more ephemeral, but it converts better. Search is more lasting. It has a sense of permanence.

But the internet has become much fuzzier in the last decade. It’s not simply search or social anymore. Traffic is fuzzy, it crosses platforms, and the entropy is very high. We can’t afford to think linearly — search or social — we need to think: internet.

The shift
User journeys are fuzzier. The destinations where people spend time and traverse the internet through are disrupted and not the same anymore. Think of the internet as a constantly shape-shifting graph. Refound maximises your share of voice in that graph.
