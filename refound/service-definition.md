## Positioning

**One-liner:** We design, build, and operate the SEO function for your team.

**Category:** Internet discovery operations — an evolution of SEO agencies.

**Core thesis:** The internet is no longer linear. User journeys don't follow "search" or "social" — they traverse a constantly shape-shifting graph of platforms, LLMs, UGC forums, aggregators, and social feeds. The entropy is high. Brands can't think in channels anymore — they need to think "internet." Refound maximises your share of voice across this graph.

**What it replaces:** The old SEO agency model — a few pages, a few backlinks, a monthly report. Refound is a continuous, always-on system that programmatically builds content, programmatically distributes links, and programmatically monitors your entire web presence.

**What it is NOT:** This is not a product/tool that helps you figure out what's going on. This is a productised service that increases your share of voice on the internet and drives higher relevant traffic to you.

## Three Arms

Refound combines three traditionally separate functions into one operated service:

### 1. Webmaster (Technical Arm)
The technical foundation. An AI-native webmaster that continuously monitors and fixes your web infrastructure.

**What it does:**
- Audits your website for indexing issues, broken links, and redirect chains
- Monitors HTML-to-text ratios, page speed, and crawlability
- Checks LLM readability — how well AI systems can parse and cite your content
- Manages sitemaps, robots.txt, canonical tags, and structured data
- Fixes issues automatically where possible, flags for human review where not
- Tracks search console data, coverage reports, and indexing health

**How it works:**
We have a framework (not a product). Based on this framework, we build a live reporting and action engine around the jobs-to-be-done of a webmaster — customised to your site, your stack, and your scale.

**Output:** Live dashboard showing site health, indexing status, issues found/fixed, and action items.

### 2. Content Engine (Data-Driven Content Arm)
Continuous SEO content publishing and maintenance — not a one-time calendar, but an always-running engine.

**What it does:**
- Automatically builds and updates content calendars based on keyword gaps, traffic data, and competitive shifts
- Pushes content drafts to your content team — blog posts, landing pages, comparison pages
- Manages programmatic SEO projects — template-driven pages at scale (100s to 1000s)
- Identifies and queues backlink opportunities — guest posts, directories, resource pages
- Monitors Reddit, Quora, and UGC platforms for relevant threads and participation opportunities
- Tracks content age, freshness, and decay — flags pieces that need updating

**How it works:**
We plug into your existing content workflow. If you have writers, we feed them briefs and drafts. If you don't, we produce the content. Either way, the engine runs continuously — not in sprints.

**Output:** Content drafts pushed on cadence, programmatic page templates deployed, backlink pipeline active.

### 3. Distribution (Social & UGC Arm)
Not social media management. Not creating posts. This is content distribution — making sure what you publish actually reaches people across platforms.

**What it does:**
- Distributes content across UGC platforms (Reddit, Quora, industry forums)
- Syndicates blog content to relevant aggregators and communities
- Builds product listings and profiles on comparison sites and directories
- Monitors brand mentions and engages in relevant conversations
- Tracks referral traffic from distribution channels

**How it works:**
Every piece of content gets a distribution plan. We don't just publish and hope — we push content to where the conversations are happening.

**Output:** Distribution reports, referral traffic growth, brand mention tracking.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Primary buyer:** Content marketer or content team lead at a B2B company where organic/search traffic is a meaningful business channel.

**Sweet spot:**
- Companies where internet journeys have high entropy — many people searching, competitive keywords, large addressable voice
- Larger ticket sizes — the ROI of continuous SEO operations justifies the investment
- Teams that already have some content function but can't scale or systematise it
- Companies that have outgrown one-off SEO audits and project-based agencies

**Industries:** B2B SaaS, D2C e-commerce, fintech, edtech, healthtech — anywhere search volume is high and competition is fierce.

**Geography:** India-first. Priced for the Indian market.

**Not for:**
- Companies that need one landing page optimised
- Very early startups with no traffic or content yet
- Companies looking for a "tool" — Refound is an operated service, not software

## Delivery Model

### Phase 1: Setup (One-time)
- Full site audit (technical + content + backlink profile)
- Framework deployment — custom webmaster dashboard, content engine setup, distribution playbook
- Baseline metrics — current traffic, rankings, indexed pages, domain authority
- Strategy document — 90-day roadmap with priorities

### Phase 2: Operations (Ongoing, monthly)
- Webmaster monitoring and fixes (continuous)
- Content calendar generation and draft delivery (weekly/bi-weekly cadence)
- Programmatic SEO projects (as identified)
- Distribution execution (ongoing)
- Monthly reporting — traffic, rankings, content published, issues resolved
